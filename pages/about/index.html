About Philly's Connect

Philadelphia is more than a historic city. It is a place shaped by people, neighborhoods, culture, and shared experiences.

Philly's Connect was created to bring together the stories, landmarks, and voices that define the city.

This site helps users explore how Philadelphia’s past and present are connected through community, identity, and local impact.

From important events to meaningful places, every part of Philly adds to the story of the city.

Through stories and community-focused pages, Philly's Connect makes that story easier to explore.
