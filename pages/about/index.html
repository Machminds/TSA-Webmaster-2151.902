About Philly's Connect

Philly's Connect is designed to highlight the people, places, and stories that make Philadelphia such a powerful cultural and historical center.

The city is shaped not only by major events and landmarks, but also by its neighborhoods, institutions, traditions, and everyday community life.

Our Purpose

This website brings together important parts of Philadelphia in one space, making it easier to explore the city’s history, identity, and lasting impact.

Why Philadelphia Matters

Philadelphia has played a major role in shaping American history while continuing to grow through art, education, culture, and strong local communities.

What You Can Discover

Visitors can explore historic landmarks, meaningful stories, community spaces, and important moments that continue to define the city today.

Built to Connect

Philly's Connect is meant to serve as a bridge between the city’s past and present, helping users understand how Philadelphia’s story is still being written.

More Than a Historic City

Philadelphia is more than a collection of famous landmarks. It is a city of communities, creativity, resilience, and shared experiences. From historic sites to neighborhood culture, every part of the city adds to its identity.

Explore Philly Further

Continue exploring the stories, places, and events that make Philadelphia one of the most influential and connected cities in the country.
