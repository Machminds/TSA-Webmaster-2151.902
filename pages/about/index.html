About Philly's Connect

Philadelphia is more than a historic city. It is shaped by neighborhoods, institutions, public spaces, and the people who live there.

Philly's Connect was created to bring local resources, meaningful stories, and community opportunities into one accessible platform.

Instead of forcing users to search across separate websites, this hub organizes trusted information in one place so support is easier to find.
