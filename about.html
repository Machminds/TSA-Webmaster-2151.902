About Downingtown Link

Downingtown Link is a student created community resource hub for residents of the Downingtown Area School District. It brings local services, non profits, and programs into one simple place.

Purpose of this Website

Many families know that help exists somewhere, but it is hard to figure out where to start. Food assistance might be on one website, mental health services on another, and youth programs on a third. Downingtown Link tries to remove that confusion.

The site gives residents a single hub where they can:

  • Search and filter a directory of community resources
  • Find support by type, such as food, housing, health care, or mental health
  • See which resources are near them using mailing address cities like Downingtown, Exton, Chester Springs, Glenmoore, and Romansville
  • Suggest new organizations through the Submit a Resource form

The goal is simple: make it easier for people to ask for help, easier for them to give help, and easier to see what already exists in the community.

How Downingtown Link Works

Interactive Resource Hub

The Resource Hub page lists community organizations with short descriptions, contact links, and locations. Visitors can use search and drop down filters to narrow results by type and city.

Featured Resources

The home page highlights a few key organizations so that users who are in a hurry can reach important services such as food banks or senior programs quickly.

Submit a Resource

Because communities change over time, the directory is not locked. Anyone can submit a new resource using the form. Submissions are reviewed before they are added to the public list.
